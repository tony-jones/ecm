Blossoms and branches

Absolutely gorgeous tattoo of cherry blossom flowers blooming off dark branches that spring up from the girl’s side.

Side and hip branch & cherry blossom tattoo

Tattoos like this one are such feminine and pretty pieces of art. The photo has real cherry blossom flowers held up to the tattoo to show how the image really does portray how delicate these flowers are in real life. The tattoo branches have black and grey detailing that come to thin ends with sweet little pink and white flower buds.

Having the tattoo start in a smaller area like the hip and become bigger along the back and side makes it look how a tree would grow naturally. An idea for the buds would be to have a few in full bloom to add a bit more color. Touches of green could be added to the thin branches in leaf shapes as well.
